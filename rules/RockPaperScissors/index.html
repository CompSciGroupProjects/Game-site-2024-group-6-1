Rock Paper Scissors

Rock Paper Scissors is an easy game with simple rules. You have three options, rock paper or scissors (each represented by a button). Once you click one, the computer will randomly make its choice. Rock beats Scissors, Paper beats Rock, and Scissors beats Paper. If you and the computer pick the same thing, it's a tie. Wanna play?
